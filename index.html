About

The IEEE Student Branch at Musaliar College of Engineering and Technology (MCET), Pathanamthitta, has been a hub of innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence since its inception on 22nd November 2012.

As a dynamic extension of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), our branch was founded with a clear mission: to create awareness among budding engineers about emerging technologies and global innovations. Over the years, IEEE SB MCET has grown into a well-structured and vibrant community, committed to empowering students through technical workshops, hackathons, and innovation challenges.
